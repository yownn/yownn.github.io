Fun Facts

There are over 34,000 recognized species of fish around the world! (And Counting)

Manta rays have the largest brain to body weight ratio of any living fish; giving it enough smarts to make the average dolphin jealous.

The fastest fish is the sailfish. It can swim as fast as a car travels on the highway.
