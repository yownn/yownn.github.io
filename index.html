Fish of The Month

This Month's Feature: The Ocean Sunfish

Renowned for its flat, bony body and massive size, the ocean sunfish or common mola (mola-mola), deserved to be featured as this month's fish of the month. The species is native to tropical and temperate waters around the world. It resembles a fish head with a tail, and its main body is flattened laterally. Sunfish can be as tall as they are long when their dorsal and ventral fins are extended.
